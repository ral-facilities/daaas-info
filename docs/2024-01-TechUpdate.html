ISIS DAaaS Tech Update - January 2024

  • ISIS Instrument Data Cache 2024
  • ISIS Specifications
  • Training

Workspaces

Rocky Linux 8 is available on all workspaces from 5 February 2024.

Many thanks to all the instrument scientists at the ISIS facility who have assisted in testing the new workspaces since September 2023.

Data

ISIS Data cache is the main data source for instrument data from 5 February 2024.

The summary of changes and follow-up actions after the Tech Update can be found in Section ISIS Instrument Data Cache 2024.

Specification

ISIS Specifications of all workspaces are here for review. Please send feedback to our support if any updates needed.

Training

Check out our Training site if you are an organizer of conferences, workshops or training courses.

To-do list from Tech Update 01/2024

  1. Cycle folders are available along with the new data structure for instrument data (Done)

  2. The workspace claim page will display available workspaces.

  3. Develop a solution for managing data with unknown RB numbers.

  4. Work out a better method to mount the ISIS archive for instrument scientists.

  5. Work out a better way to help instrument scientists setting up Mantid default directories.

  6. ISIS research groups are to review their workspace specs.
